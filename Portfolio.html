About

My first taste of web development started when I was a kid when I made my first website (a Petz game tribute) using MS FrontPage and hosting through GeoCities. Technology changed greatly, and so did my interests (still love animals though!). Overtime, I kept returning to the thought of web development, and after stints creating art and a degree in Marketing, I have found my home learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.


Due to my background in Marketing, I am highly aware of the importance of brand development and creating a great customer experience. Web development provides me a space to bring all of my passions together and I promise to bring that same passion to each new endeavor!


My favorite thing about coding is that there are always methods to improve and new technologies to learn. I've recently tackled learning React and right now, I am currently working on back-end technologies such as Node.js, Express, and MongoDB in order to create dynamic full-stack applications.


Feel free to look through my current work below and if you'd like to work together, please let me know! I can't wait to see what amazing things we create.
